ART 151

FAMINE

You have never felt more hungry in your life.

You can hear your stomach eating itself.

Famine grins and the ground beneath you shakes.

"I am not like WAR, DEATH nor PESTILENCE." Famine says.

"I will grant you a feast."

You know there is a catch somewhere, but agree to the offer.

You may choose one of the following options or I send you to my brother.

  • DESSERT DELIGHT
  • EXOTIC CUISINE
  • GO TO PESTILENCE
